# Business Glossary

## Financial Metrics
- **LTV (Life Time Value)**: Total revenue expected from a customer.
  - Formula: `Avg Order Value * Purchase Frequency * Lifespan`
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)**: Cost to acquire a customer.
  - Formula: `Total Marketing Spend / New Customers Acquired`
- **ROE (Return on Equity)**: Net Income / Shareholder's Equity.
- **Net Profit Margin**: (Revenue - Cost) / Revenue.

## Product Metrics
- **MAU (Monthly Active Users)**: Unique users who performed an action in the last 30 days.
- **Churn Rate**: % of customers who stopped using the product in a given period.
  - Definition of "Stopped": No activity for 90 days.
