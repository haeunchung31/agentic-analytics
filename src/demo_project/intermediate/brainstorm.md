# Problem: Q4 Revenue Drop for Product X

## Key Question(s)
Why has revenue for Product X dropped in Q4 compared to Q3?

## Context
- **Observation**: 15-20% drop in revenue starting October.
- **Data Available**: Daily sales data by region.

## Hypotheses
1.  **Seasonality**: Product X is a seasonal product with naturally lower demand in Q4.
2.  **Pricing/Volume**: The drop is driven by lower volume (units sold) rather than price changes.
